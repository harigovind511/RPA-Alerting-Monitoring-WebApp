What is NLP?

Natural Language Processing or NLP is a way for computers to analyze, understand, and derive meaning from human language in a smart and useful way. By utilizing NLP, developers can organize and structure knowledge to perform tasks such as automatic summarization, translation, named entity recognition, relationship extraction, sentiment analysis, speech recognition, and topic segmentation. At Automation Factory, we use a combination of several cognitive tools to provide solutions that not only understand and comprehend natural language of Unilever users, but translates this language into usable actions and decisions. For example, we have developed an engine called the Knowledge Based Smart Search that reads and smartly comprehends data within Unilever documents. It then makes all the information searchable with the use of a simple natural language query. Feel free to try out and test some of our solutions provided below with your own data.
